Traditional RSVPs — reply cards, email threads, phone calls — create friction for guests and spreadsheet chaos for organisers. An RSVP poll replaces all of that with one link, three clicks, and a live headcount.

Free tier: VoteGenerator's RSVP poll is free for events up to 100 guests. For larger events, Pro at $19/month raises the limit to 10,000 responses. Most parties, team events, and small weddings fall comfortably within the free tier.

How to Create an RSVP Poll

Choose RSVP from the 8 available poll types. It gives you the Yes / No / Maybe format built for event attendance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to how many people can RSVP?
VoteGenerator's free tier allows up to 100 responses per poll. For events with more than 100 guests — larger weddings, corporate all-hands, or big community events — a Pro plan at $19/month raises the limit to 10,000 responses per poll.
Do guests need to sign up or create an account?
No. Guests click the link, see the three options (Yes / No / Maybe), click one, and they are done. No account, no email address, no download. This is the main reason RSVP polls get higher response rates than traditional RSVP forms.
Can I track who responded and who did not?
VoteGenerator's RSVP poll tracks the total count per option — how many Yes, No, and Maybe — but not individual names. It tells you "52 people said Yes" rather than "these 52 people." For events needing named responses (seating charts, name badges), combine the RSVP poll for quick headcount with a follow-up form asking for names.
Can someone change their RSVP after responding?
Yes. Guests who respond from the same device can change their response at any time — useful when plans change. Encourage guests to update their response if their availability changes rather than letting a "Yes" become a no-show.
How long should I leave the RSVP poll open?
Weddings: 4–6 weeks before the event. Corporate events: 2–3 weeks. Parties: 1–2 weeks. Set the poll to auto-close on your deadline — this creates a natural urgency and stops you from manually closing it. Send a reminder message 2–3 days before it closes.
Can I add more questions beyond Yes / No / Maybe?
RSVP polls are intentionally simple — one question, three answers. This is what makes them so fast for guests. For additional information (dietary requirements, plus-ones, song requests), use VoteGenerator's Survey poll type alongside the RSVP, or link to a separate form.
