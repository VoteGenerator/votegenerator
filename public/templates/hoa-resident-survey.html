FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this HOA resident survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no signup required. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses and a response timeline for comparing maintenance scores, communication ratings, and NPS year over year.
Should HOA resident surveys be anonymous?
Yes, in most cases. Anonymous surveys produce significantly more candid responses about maintenance quality and management responsiveness, topics residents might hesitate to raise directly. VoteGenerator surveys are anonymous by default.
How do I share the survey with residents?
Include the link in your HOA newsletter or email, share in your community app or messaging group, or display a QR code on notice boards. Residents respond on any device without creating an account. Many boards also send a reminder a week before the deadline to maximise response rates.
How often should we run a resident survey?
Annual surveys before the budget planning cycle are the most common approach, giving residents a structured voice before priorities and dues are set. A shorter three-question pulse check after a major project or policy change helps measure resident response quickly.
Can this be used for condo associations and apartment communities?
Yes. Works for any residential community association including HOAs, condo associations, co-ops, and build-to-rent communities. Adjust the wording of the maintenance and amenity questions to match your specific property type.
What happens when I reach 100 responses?
VoteGenerator will notify you as you approach the limit. Pro ($19/month) gives you up to 10,000 responses, a year-over-year comparison timeline, and CSV export for reporting to your board or management company.
