FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this online course feedback survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no student signup required. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses and comparison across course iterations.
When should I send the survey to students?
At course completion, ideally within 24 to 48 hours of finishing the final lesson. For long courses, a mid-course pulse check at the halfway point catches issues before students drop out.
How is this different from a standard course evaluation?
A standard course evaluation is designed for institutional teaching in a classroom setting. This template is built specifically for online and self-paced courses: it includes a dedicated question on platform and technical experience, separates learning objective achievement from general content quality, and is mobile-first for students who completed the course on their phone.
Can I use this for corporate e-learning and LMS courses?
Yes. The template works for Udemy, Teachable, Kajabi, Thinkific, and corporate LMS platforms. Adjust the platform question to reference your specific LMS, and replace the value question with relevance to job responsibilities for corporate learning contexts.
What's the most important question to watch?
Question 6: whether students achieved the learning objectives the course promised. High content scores paired with a low objectives score is the most common driver of refund requests and negative reviews. It means students enjoyed the content but didn't get the outcome they paid for, which is a sales copy or course structure issue, not a quality issue.
