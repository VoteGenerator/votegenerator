FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this product feedback survey template free?
Yes. Free forever, no signup from you or your users. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses for large-scale product feedback campaigns.
What questions should a product feedback survey include?
The most actionable product surveys cover: overall satisfaction, NPS, usage frequency, most valued features, ease of use, unmet needs, top improvement request, and open feedback. This template covers all eight dimensions.
When should I send a product feedback survey?
Send after meaningful product usage — at least a few sessions, not immediately after signup. In-product prompts triggered after key actions work best. Email surveys work well quarterly for relationship NPS. Avoid sending during onboarding flows.
How do I get more users to complete the survey?
Keep it under 5 minutes (this template takes 3-4 minutes). Trigger it at a moment of success — after completing a task, not at a friction point. "Your answers directly shape our roadmap" increases completion rates meaningfully.
What is the difference between product feedback and CSAT?
CSAT measures satisfaction with your service as a whole. Product feedback goes deeper into specific product dimensions — feature value, usability, unmet needs, roadmap priorities. Use our CSAT template for business-level health; use this template to inform your product roadmap.
How do I use product feedback to prioritise my roadmap?
Question 7 ('What is the one thing you'd most like us to improve?') is your most direct roadmap signal. Cross-reference with Q4 (most valued features) and Q6 (unmet needs) to find the intersection of what users love and what's missing. High-frequency Q7 answers are your highest-confidence roadmap bets.
Can I export product feedback data?
Free accounts can download a visual screenshot. Pro ($19/month) includes CSV and Excel export — structured data you can load into your analytics tool, share with engineering, or use for investor updates.
How is this different from tools like Pendo or Hotjar?
In-app tools embed directly in your product UI. VoteGenerator is a shareable link — ideal for email surveys, Slack outreach, user interview follow-ups, or any context where you can't or don't want to instrument your product. No code required from you; no login from your users.
