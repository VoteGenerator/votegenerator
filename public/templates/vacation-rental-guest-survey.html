FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this vacation rental guest survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no signup required from you or your guests. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses, better for hosts managing multiple properties or collecting feedback continuously.
Does this work for Airbnb, VRBO, and other platforms?
Yes. The template works for any short-term rental platform or direct booking property. Share via link or QR code in the property. Guests don't need an account to respond.
When should I send the survey to guests?
Within 24 hours of checkout while the stay is still fresh. Many hosts include the link in their departure message. Waiting more than 48 hours typically reduces completion rates significantly.
Are guest responses anonymous?
By default yes. Anonymous surveys tend to produce more candid feedback about issues guests might hesitate to raise directly. You can optionally add a contact field if you want to follow up with specific guests.
How does this help with public reviews?
Guests who complete a private feedback survey and feel heard are significantly more likely to leave a positive public review. The survey also flags issues you can address before a dissatisfied guest posts publicly.
Can I customise the questions for my property?
Yes. Every question is fully editable. Add questions about specific amenities, parking, a pool or hot tub, or local recommendations. Most hosts are done customising in under two minutes.
