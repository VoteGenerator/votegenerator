What is a CSAT survey?
CSAT stands for Customer Satisfaction Score. It measures how satisfied customers are with your product, service, or interaction. One of three core CX metrics alongside NPS and CES.
What is a good CSAT score?
CSAT above 80% (counting 4s and 5s as satisfied) is good. Above 90% is excellent. SaaS typically targets 85-90%, e-commerce 75-85%. Track your own trend — improvement matters more than hitting a specific number.
What is the difference between NPS and CSAT?
NPS measures loyalty and likelihood to recommend — a leading indicator of growth. CSAT measures satisfaction with a specific interaction — a snapshot of current quality. NPS predicts future behaviour; CSAT diagnoses current experience. This template captures both in 5 questions.
