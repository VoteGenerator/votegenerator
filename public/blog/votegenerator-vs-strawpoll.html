Overview

These two tools are not interchangeable. VoteGenerator wins on poll type depth — 8 types including ranked choice and meeting scheduling that StrawPoll simply does not have. StrawPoll wins on free response volume — unlimited responses on the free tier versus VoteGenerator's 100-response cap. Which advantage matters more depends entirely on what you are trying to do.

StrawPoll has been running since 2009 and has hosted tens of millions of polls. It is the familiar name in quick community polling, particularly in Discord servers and online communities. The free tier is genuinely generous.

VoteGenerator is newer and built around a wider range of poll formats. The key differentiator is poll type depth — you can run a ranked choice election, a Doodle-style scheduling poll, a visual poll where respondents pick between images, and a dot-voting prioritisation session, all for free. The 100-response cap is the significant tradeoff.

The Verdict

Bottom Line
Different tools for different jobs — the right one is the one that matches your constraint
If you need ranked choice voting, meeting scheduling, rating scales, or anything beyond basic multiple choice, VoteGenerator is the clear answer. No other free poll tool ships 8 poll types including ranked choice, Doodle-style scheduling, visual image polls, and dot voting. For teams, educators, event organisers, and anyone collecting structured feedback, this variety is genuinely useful.

If you need to reach more than 100 people for free, StrawPoll is the practical choice. Its unlimited free tier is a real advantage that VoteGenerator cannot match without a paid plan. For Discord communities, subreddit polls, or any audience in the hundreds or thousands, StrawPoll's free capacity wins.

The two tools are not in direct competition for the same job. VoteGenerator is for structured, varied polling where format matters. StrawPoll is for simple, high-volume community voting where format is secondary to reach.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is VoteGenerator better than StrawPoll?
It depends on what you need. VoteGenerator is better for poll type variety — ranked choice voting, meeting scheduling, rating scales, and 6 other formats that StrawPoll does not have. StrawPoll is better for response volume — its free tier is unlimited, while VoteGenerator's free tier caps at 100 responses per poll. Neither tool is objectively better; the right choice depends on whether poll format or free response capacity matters more to you.
Does VoteGenerator have unlimited free responses?
No. VoteGenerator's free plan allows up to 100 responses per poll and 3 active polls simultaneously. If you need more than 100 responses, you can use StrawPoll's free tier (unlimited) or upgrade to VoteGenerator Pro at $19/month, which raises the limit to 10,000 responses per poll and adds CSV export, geographic stats, and device breakdown.
Does VoteGenerator have ranked choice voting?
Yes. Ranked choice voting using an instant runoff algorithm is built into VoteGenerator and available on the free tier. Voters drag options to rank them by preference. Results show round-by-round elimination until a winner emerges. StrawPoll does not offer ranked choice voting.
What is the best StrawPoll alternative?
VoteGenerator is the strongest alternative if you need more poll types — especially ranked choice voting, meeting scheduling polls (Doodle-style), rating scales, or visual polls with image options. All 8 poll types are free. If you specifically need unlimited free responses, StrawPoll is hard to beat on that metric alone.
Can I use VoteGenerator for anonymous polls?
Yes. VoteGenerator polls are anonymous by default. Respondents do not need to create an account or log in. IP addresses are not stored in poll results. StrawPoll also supports anonymous voting. Both tools show aggregate results only — vote counts and percentages, not individual voter information.
Can I export my poll results?
VoteGenerator offers PNG chart export free on all plans. CSV export of raw response data requires a paid plan (Pro at $19/month). StrawPoll's free export options are limited. If data export is important to you, VoteGenerator Pro is the stronger option between the two.
Is StrawPoll completely free?
StrawPoll's core poll creation is free with unlimited responses. They offer a premium tier with additional features, but the free tier has no response caps. This is StrawPoll's primary advantage over VoteGenerator's free plan.
