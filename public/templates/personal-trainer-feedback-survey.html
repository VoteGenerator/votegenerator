FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this personal trainer feedback survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no client signup required. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses, useful for PTs managing large client rosters or multi-trainer studios tracking performance across coaches.
When should I send the survey to clients?
Three moments work best: at 30 days when a new client has enough sessions to give meaningful feedback, at 90 days before the first package renewal decision, and at the end of each programme block. Annual surveys for long-term clients track satisfaction over time.
Should I make it anonymous?
Usually not fully anonymous for personal training, since the feedback is about a specific coaching relationship. However, many clients are more candid about programme issues in a structured survey than face to face. Most PTs share the link via WhatsApp with a message framing it as a quality check for their own development.
How does client feedback help grow my PT business?
Clients who complete a feedback survey and feel heard are significantly more likely to refer a friend. The NPS question directly identifies your promoters: clients who score 9 or 10 are statistically likely to mention you to someone in their network without being asked. One referred client from a satisfied long-term client is worth far more than most paid advertising.
Can I use this for online coaching clients?
Yes. Questions are format-agnostic. For online clients, adjust the session quality question to cover programme delivery and check-in quality. The goal-setting, motivation, and results questions apply equally to online and in-person coaching relationships.
What happens when I reach 100 responses?
Pro ($19/month) gives you up to 10,000 responses, a timeline comparing satisfaction across client cohorts, and CSV export for business tracking.
