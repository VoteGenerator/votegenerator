FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this team building survey template free?
Yes. Free forever. Up to 100 anonymous responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses and comparison across events over time.
Should team building surveys be anonymous?
Yes. Anonymous surveys produce more candid responses about inclusion and whether activities built genuine connection, topics employees might hesitate to raise if their manager sees individual responses.
When should I send the survey?
Within 24 to 48 hours of the activity. Sending the following week loses the emotional context. For virtual activities, send at the close of the session.
Can this be used for virtual team building activities?
Yes. Questions work for both in-person and virtual formats. For virtual events, the inclusion question is particularly valuable since remote participants sometimes feel disconnected from the group experience.
How does this help justify the team building budget?
Question 6 directly measures whether employees felt the investment was worthwhile. Sharing aggregate scores alongside engagement metrics helps HR make data-driven decisions about which formats to repeat and how to plan future events.
