When to Send

Timing Is Everything

Experience data

24–48 hours after purchase

Captures how the buying experience felt — browsing, checkout, trust. Memory is freshest right after the order confirmation.

Product data

7–14 days after delivery

Captures actual product satisfaction after use. This is when "would you buy again?" becomes a meaningful signal rather than optimistic guessing.

This template is designed for post-delivery sending — it asks about product quality (Q4) which needs hands-on time to answer honestly. Send 7–10 days after expected delivery for the highest quality responses.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this post-purchase survey template free?
Yes. Free forever, no signup from you or your customers. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses and CSV export for your CX team.
When should I send a post-purchase survey?
Send within 24-48 hours of purchase for experience feedback, or 7-14 days after expected delivery for product quality data. This template is designed for post-delivery — send it 7-10 days after expected delivery for the best responses.
What questions should a post-purchase survey include?
The most valuable questions cover: overall experience, ease of finding products, checkout satisfaction, product quality, recommendation likelihood (NPS), repurchase intent, and an open text. This template covers all seven.
How do I share this survey with customers?
You get a shareable link. Add it to your post-purchase email sequence, order confirmation page, shipping notification, or SMS. No login required from customers — they click and respond immediately.
Can I integrate this with Shopify or WooCommerce?
VoteGenerator generates a simple shareable link which you can embed anywhere — Shopify order confirmation emails, WooCommerce thank you pages, Klaviyo flows, or any email platform. No plugin required. Just paste the link.
How do I track repeat purchase rates from survey data?
Question 6 ("Would you buy from us again?") is your repurchase intent indicator. Pro ($19/month) includes CSV export so you can cross-reference survey responses with actual repeat purchase data in your CRM or analytics.
