Why Look for Mentimeter Alternatives?

Mentimeter's free tier typically allows only 2 quiz slides and 5 quick slides per presentation. For anything beyond a very brief demo, you hit the wall quickly. Paid plans start around $11.99–$24.99/month — reasonable for regular use, but not if you present once a year or are on a tight budget.

Mentimeter free tier limitations
  • Limited to ~2 quiz slides per presentation
  • Limited to ~5 quick slides per presentation
  • Signup required to create
  • No ranked choice or meeting polls
  • Paid plans from $11.99/month
What to look for in an alternative
  • Functional free tier without per-slide limits
  • Real-time audience results display
  • No respondent signup required
  • Works on any device in the audience
  • Reasonable free response limits

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best free alternative to Mentimeter?
VoteGenerator is the strongest option if you want no signup required and a wide variety of poll types including ranked choice, meeting scheduling, and visual polls. If you need slides built into the tool alongside polls, AhaSlides is the closest Mentimeter replacement at a lower price. For professional conferences with live Q&A, Slido is the best-in-class alternative.
Is Mentimeter really free?
Mentimeter has a free tier but it is very restricted — typically around 2 quiz slides and 5 quick slides per presentation. For any real presentation, you will hit this limit. Paid plans start around $11.99–$24.99 per month. It is more accurate to call Mentimeter free tier a demo than a working free product.
Can I use VoteGenerator for a live event presentation?
Yes. Create a poll before the event, project the results URL to your screen, and share the voting URL with the audience (via QR code or a short link). Results update in real time as people vote. VoteGenerator does not integrate into PowerPoint or Google Slides — you would display it in a browser tab alongside your presentation. For events under 100 people, the free tier covers this completely.
Which Mentimeter alternative requires no account to use?
VoteGenerator is the only tool in this comparison that requires no account from the creator or the audience. Every other tool (Slido, AhaSlides, Poll Everywhere, Kahoot) requires at least a creator account. For audience members, most tools in this list do not require respondents to sign up.
What is the easiest Mentimeter alternative to set up?
VoteGenerator: no account, create in under 60 seconds, share a link. Kahoot is next — quick to set up once you have an account, familiar game-based interface. AhaSlides and Slido take a bit more initial setup but are well-designed. Poll Everywhere has a slightly steeper learning curve than the others.
