All 8 Survey Questions

Eight questions capturing the full picture of a speaker's performance: quality of content, quality of delivery, and whether attendees actually got something useful out of it. Question 6, whether the key message was clear, is the most predictive of whether attendees will apply what they heard. High content scores with a low clarity score almost always points to pacing or structure issues, not the ideas themselves.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this speaker evaluation survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no signup required from you or your attendees. Up to 100 responses per survey free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses, better for large conference sessions running evaluations simultaneously.
When should I share the speaker evaluation survey?
Immediately at the end of the session while the talk is still fresh. Display the QR code on the speaker's final slide so attendees can scan before they leave. For virtual events, post the link in the chat at the end. Waiting more than an hour significantly reduces response quality.
Can I use this for keynotes, breakout sessions, and workshops?
Yes. The template works for any presentation type. Adjust the delivery question to cover facilitation quality for workshops, or the pacing question to cover moderator effectiveness for panel sessions. Customisation takes under two minutes.
Can speakers see their own results?
You control who sees the results. VoteGenerator gives you a shareable results link you can choose to pass on to the speaker after the event. Many organisers share aggregate scores as post-event feedback to help speakers improve for future engagements.
How do I run evaluations for multiple speakers at the same event?
Create a separate survey for each speaker or session using the same template. Add the speaker name and session title before each session. Each QR code links to the correct survey. VoteGenerator Pro lets you run unlimited simultaneous surveys.
What happens when I reach 100 responses?
VoteGenerator will notify you as you approach the limit. Pro ($19/month) gives you up to 10,000 responses per survey, a comparison view across sessions, and CSV export for post-event reporting.
