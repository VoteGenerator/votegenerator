FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this school parent survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no signup required from you or the parents responding. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses and a year-on-year comparison timeline.
What questions should a school parent satisfaction survey include?
The most valuable parent survey questions cover: overall school satisfaction, teaching quality, school-home communication, student safety and wellbeing, extracurricular opportunities, individual support, confidence in leadership, NPS, and open feedback. This template covers all nine dimensions.
How do I share the survey with parents?
Include the link in your parent newsletter, school app notification, or email. Display a QR code at parents evenings and open days. Parents respond on any device without creating an account. Send during term time for best response rates.
Should the survey be anonymous for parents?
Yes, in most cases. Anonymous surveys produce significantly more candid responses about teaching quality and school leadership. VoteGenerator surveys are anonymous by default. You can optionally add a contact field for follow-up conversations.
When is the best time to run a school parent survey?
Annual surveys work best in the spring term before end-of-year planning. A mid-autumn check-in after new year groups have settled is also common. Avoid the final two weeks of any term when response rates drop sharply.
What happens when I reach 100 responses?
VoteGenerator will notify you as you approach the limit. Pro ($19/month) gives you up to 10,000 responses, a year-on-year comparison timeline, and CSV export for governing body reporting.
