FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this event feedback survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no signup from you or your attendees. Up to 100 responses free — perfect for smaller events and webinars. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses for large conferences.
What questions should an event feedback survey include?
Effective event surveys cover: overall rating, content relevance, speaker quality, venue or platform experience, event length, networking opportunities, likelihood to attend again, future topics, and open feedback. This template covers all nine areas.
When should I send an event feedback survey?
Send within 24 hours of the event ending — ideally the same day or following morning. Response rates drop significantly after 48 hours. For multi-day conferences, send a daily micro-survey after each day plus a comprehensive one at the end.
Does this work for virtual events and webinars?
Yes. The template works for both in-person and virtual events. For webinars, the venue question applies to the platform experience. All questions are fully editable before sharing — customise them to match your specific event format.
How do I share this survey with attendees?
You get a shareable link. Show it on your closing slide as a QR code, include it in your post-event email, drop it in the event platform chat, or send via SMS. No login required from attendees — they click and respond immediately.
Can I use this for internal team events?
Yes. This template works well for internal events like team offsites, all-hands meetings, training sessions, and company retreats. The anonymous response option is especially valuable for internal events where attendees may hesitate to give candid feedback in person.
