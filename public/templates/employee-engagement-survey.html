Why This Template

Why Use an Employee Engagement Survey?

Employee engagement directly predicts retention, productivity, and profitability. Gallup research consistently finds that highly engaged business units achieve 23% higher profitability and 18% higher productivity compared to disengaged ones. But you can only improve what you measure.

This template covers the six dimensions that research identifies as the core drivers of engagement: motivation, manager quality, resources, recognition, cultural alignment, and growth opportunity. One open-ended question at the end captures anything the rating scale questions miss.

The questions are direct enough to give you actionable data, but neutral enough to feel safe for honest answers. Anonymity is the key — VoteGenerator never stores names or emails, so employees can respond candidly without worrying about identification.

Best practice: Run this survey quarterly and share results transparently with your team. Employees who see their feedback lead to real changes participate at significantly higher rates in future surveys.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this employee engagement survey template free?
Yes, completely free. No signup required from you or your employees. VoteGenerator's free plan allows up to 100 responses per survey. For teams larger than 100 people, Pro ($19/month) allows up to 10,000 responses per survey.
Can I customise the survey questions?
Yes. After clicking "Use This Template", every question is fully editable. Add, remove, or reword any question before launching. Change the rating scale, add sections, or replace questions entirely — the template is just a starting point.
Will employees know who submitted what?
No. VoteGenerator does not store names or email addresses. Responses are anonymous by default. No login is required to respond, so there's no way to link a response to an individual. Employees can answer honestly without fear of identification.
How do I export the survey results?
Free accounts can download a PNG screenshot of results. Pro accounts ($19/month) can export to CSV or Excel for deeper analysis in spreadsheets, or for sharing with HR leadership in a formal report format.
How often should I run this survey?
Research recommends running a full engagement survey quarterly or annually, combined with a shorter pulse check (3-5 questions) monthly. Running this template every quarter gives you trend data to see whether changes you make are actually improving engagement scores.
Can I restrict access to invited employees only?
Free surveys are accessible to anyone with the link. Pro plan includes PIN code access, which lets you set a password so only employees you share the code with can respond — useful for controlling participation in sensitive workplace surveys.
