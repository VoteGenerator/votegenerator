Why This Template

Satisfaction vs Engagement — and Why It Matters

Job Satisfaction

How happy employees are with specific aspects of their job — pay, balance, management. The employment deal. You can have satisfied employees who are not particularly motivated.

Employee Engagement

Emotional commitment and discretionary effort — whether employees go beyond the minimum. About passion for the work. Satisfaction is a prerequisite, not the same thing.

If satisfaction is low, engagement initiatives won't work. Fix the structural issues — compensation, balance, management — before investing in engagement programs. Run this survey first to know where you actually stand.

Question 7 ("How likely are you to still be working here in 12 months?") is the strongest early warning signal for voluntary turnover. Low scores here require immediate attention regardless of other scores.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this employee satisfaction survey template free?
Yes. Free forever, no signup required from you or respondents. The free plan allows up to 100 responses per survey. For teams over 100 people, Pro ($19/month) allows up to 10,000 responses.
What's the difference between satisfaction and engagement?
Satisfaction measures how happy employees are with specific job aspects — pay, balance, management. Engagement measures emotional commitment and discretionary effort. Satisfaction is a prerequisite for engagement. If satisfaction is low, engagement programs won't fix it — address the structural issues first.
Are responses anonymous?
Yes. VoteGenerator stores no names or email addresses. Respondents need no account. Anonymous by default — essential for honest answers on sensitive topics like compensation and management quality.
Can I track satisfaction trends over time?
Free accounts see current-cycle results only. Pro accounts ($19/month) include response timeline analytics — the data you need to see whether your HR initiatives are actually moving the needle between survey cycles.
How many questions can I add or change?
The template comes with 8 questions. Free accounts support up to 10, so you can add 2 more. Pro allows up to 25 questions. Everything is fully editable after clicking Use This Template.
How do I share the survey with my team?
After using the template you get a unique shareable link. Paste it into email, Slack, or Teams. No login required from anyone who responds — they click and answer immediately.
