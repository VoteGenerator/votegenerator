Frequently Asked Questions

Is this 360 feedback survey template free?
Yes. Free forever, no signup from you or your raters. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses — better for large organisations running reviews across multiple employees simultaneously.
What is a 360 degree feedback survey?
A 360 survey collects anonymous performance assessments from multiple sources — manager, peers, and direct reports — giving a more complete picture than a single manager review. The "360 degree" refers to gathering feedback from all directions around the person.
Who should complete a 360 feedback survey?
A typical 360 review includes 4–8 raters: the direct manager, 3–5 peers, and 2–4 direct reports (if applicable). The person being assessed often completes a self-assessment using the same questions for self-awareness comparison.
Should 360 feedback be anonymous?
Yes — anonymity is essential, particularly from peers and direct reports who may fear career consequences. This anonymous feedback survey stores no names or email addresses. Responses should only be shared as aggregated themes, never attributed to individual raters.
How is 360 feedback different from a performance review?
A performance review is a single-rater assessment against objectives. A 360 review is multi-rater, gathering behavioural feedback from multiple perspectives. 360 feedback is best used for professional development. This peer feedback survey template gives you the multi-rater data to have those conversations with confidence. Traditional performance reviews are used for compensation and promotion decisions.
