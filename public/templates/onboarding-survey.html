FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this onboarding survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no signup from you or your new hires. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses — useful for organisations running multiple onboarding cohorts.
When should I send an onboarding survey?
Send at three points: end of Week 1 for first impressions, Day 30 for early experience, and Day 90 for role confidence. A three-touchpoint approach catches problems while they're still fixable.
What questions should an onboarding survey include?
The most valuable onboarding questions cover: overall experience, Day 1 readiness, team welcome, Day 1 access to tools, role clarity, manager support, culture understanding, and confidence for success. This template covers all eight areas in 10 questions.
Should onboarding surveys be anonymous?
Yes. Anonymous surveys produce more honest answers, particularly about manager effectiveness. New employees are especially reluctant to give negative feedback early in their tenure. VoteGenerator stores no names or email addresses by default.
How do I use onboarding feedback to reduce early turnover?
Look for patterns across cohorts, not individual responses. Low Day 30 scores on role clarity and manager support are the strongest predictors of first-year turnover. Address systemic issues — unclear job descriptions, inconsistent manager onboarding — before they compound across hiring waves.
Can I customise the questions for my company?
Yes. All 10 questions are fully editable after clicking Use This Template. Free accounts support up to 10 questions. Pro ($19/month) allows up to 25 — useful for adding department-specific or role-specific onboarding questions.
