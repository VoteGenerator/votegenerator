FAQ

What are "this or that" questions?
This or that questions give two options and force you to pick one — "Coffee or tea?", "Beach or mountains?". They are binary, fast, and instantly generate opinions. The forced-choice format is what makes them so effective for icebreakers, games, and social media engagement.
Are these good for work team icebreakers?
Yes — they are one of the most popular uses. They are non-threatening, light, and reveal personality without anything too personal. Pick one question from the Work category above or use the "Surprise Me" button for a random one. Takes about 2 minutes at the start of a meeting.
What if someone says "both" or "neither"?
That is the constraint. They have to pick one, even if it is nearly impossible. "If you absolutely had to choose…" is a useful framing. The inability to pick is usually where the most interesting conversations come from.
How do I create a poll from one of these questions?
Click "Poll it" on any question card. This opens VoteGenerator with the This or That poll type pre-selected. Add your two options, create the poll, and share the link. Free tier allows up to 100 responses per poll with no signup from anyone who votes.
