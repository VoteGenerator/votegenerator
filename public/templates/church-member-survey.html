FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this church member survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no signup required. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses and a response timeline, useful for larger congregations or tracking how scores change after ministry changes.
Should the survey be anonymous for church members?
Yes, in most cases. Anonymous surveys produce significantly more candid responses about pastoral care and belonging, topics members might hesitate to raise face to face, especially in smaller congregations where everyone knows each other. VoteGenerator surveys are anonymous by default.
How do I share the survey with congregation members?
Include the link in your church newsletter, email, or messaging group. Display a QR code on the screen during announcements or print it in a bulletin. Members respond on any device without needing to create an account.
When is the best time to run a church member survey?
Annual surveys before your ministry planning season, typically in autumn. A shorter pulse check after a major change, new service time, pastoral transition, or building project, helps measure how the congregation is responding before the annual meeting.
Can I customise the questions for our denomination?
Yes. Every question is fully editable. Adjust language to match your tradition, add questions about a specific programme or current change, and remove anything that doesn't apply. Used by Baptist, Anglican, Catholic, evangelical, and nondenominational churches.
What happens when I reach 100 responses?
VoteGenerator will notify you as you approach the limit. Pro ($19/month) gives you up to 10,000 responses, a response timeline for year-over-year comparison, and CSV export for reporting to church leadership or a board.
