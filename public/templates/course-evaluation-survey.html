FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this course evaluation survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no signup from you or your students. Up to 100 responses free — suitable for most class sizes. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses for large lecture courses.
What questions should a course evaluation survey include?
Effective evaluations cover: overall rating, content relevance, instructor quality, course organisation and clarity, pace, most helpful resources, likelihood to recommend, what learners will apply, and improvement feedback. This template covers all nine dimensions.
When should I send a course evaluation survey?
Send at the end of the course or final session — not during it. For multi-week courses, a mid-course check at the halfway point can flag issues while there's still time to adjust. Final evaluations sent within 24 hours of course completion get the best response rates.
Should course evaluations be anonymous?
Yes. Anonymous evaluations consistently produce more honest feedback, particularly about instructor effectiveness and course difficulty. Students who feel their grade might be affected will self-censor. VoteGenerator stores no names or email addresses by default.
Does this work for online courses and corporate training?
Yes. Works for university courses, online courses, corporate training, workshops, and bootcamps. All questions are editable — replace "instructor" with "trainer" or "facilitator" and "course" with "programme" or "workshop" before sharing.
How do I use course evaluation data for accreditation?
Pro ($19/month) includes CSV and Excel export — the structured data format required for most accreditation reporting. Aggregate across multiple cohorts to demonstrate consistent quality improvement over time.
Can I run evaluations for multiple courses?
Yes. Free accounts can run up to 3 active surveys simultaneously — suitable for most teaching loads. Each course gets its own unique link. Pro allows unlimited simultaneous active surveys.
