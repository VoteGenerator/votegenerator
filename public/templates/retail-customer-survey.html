FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this retail customer survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no signup from you or your customers. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses and a response timeline for tracking satisfaction across locations and over time.
How do I share the survey with customers in a physical store?
Display a QR code at the checkout desk, on receipts, or near the exit. Customers scan it on their phone and complete the survey in under two minutes without downloading anything. Many retailers also include the link in their post-purchase email within 24 hours of the visit.
Can I use this for both in-store and online retail?
Yes. The template is fully editable. For ecommerce, adjust the atmosphere question to cover website experience and the staff question to cover customer service response time. Both are quick edits in the builder.
When should I ask customers to complete the survey?
In-store: at checkout or near the exit while the experience is still fresh. Online: in the post-purchase confirmation email or a follow-up 24 to 48 hours after delivery. Response rates drop significantly after 72 hours.
Can I track results across multiple store locations?
Create a separate survey for each location using the same template, or add a question asking which store the customer visited. VoteGenerator Pro gives you a response timeline so you can compare how scores change across locations over time.
What happens when I reach 100 responses?
VoteGenerator will notify you as you approach the limit. Pro ($19/month) gives you up to 10,000 responses, a timeline showing how satisfaction changes over time, and CSV export for management reporting.
