All 9 Survey Questions

Members who score this 3 or below, especially when paired with a low community score, are there purely for the desk. FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this coworking space survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no signup required. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses, better for spaces with larger memberships or multi-location operators comparing satisfaction across sites.
How often should I survey coworking space members?
Quarterly pulse surveys work well for most spaces. An annual deeper survey provides a benchmark. Many operators also run a targeted send to members approaching their renewal date 4 to 6 weeks in advance, giving the team time to address dissatisfaction before the renewal decision.
Should coworking surveys be anonymous?
It depends on your community. Anonymous surveys produce more candid responses about infrastructure and value-for-money issues. For spaces with a strong community feel, many operators add an optional name field so they can follow up personally with members who flag specific issues.
What is the most important question?
Question 8, renewal intent. Members who score this 3 or below, especially when paired with a low community score (Q3), are there for the desk alone. That makes them highly price-sensitive and at risk of leaving for any cheaper alternative. Flag these members for a personal check-in within one week.
Can I use this for multi-location coworking brands?
Yes. Create a separate survey for each location using the same template, or add a "which location do you work from" question to segment results. VoteGenerator Pro lets you run and compare unlimited surveys simultaneously.
