Frequently Asked Questions

Are these poll templates really free?
Yes. All 30 templates are free to use in VoteGenerator with no account required. The free tier allows up to 100 responses per poll. For polls expecting more than 100 voters, a Pro plan at $19/month allows up to 10,000 responses per poll. All 8 poll types — including ranked choice, meeting scheduling, RSVP, rating scale, and visual polls — are free on every plan.
How do I use a template?
Click "Launch" on any card to open VoteGenerator with the poll type pre-selected. Or copy the question text with the "Copy Q" button and paste it into a new poll manually. Either way, your poll is ready to share in under 60 seconds — no account needed from you or anyone who votes.
Can I customise a template after launching it?
Yes, completely. Change the question to your specific situation, add or remove options, change the poll type, or set a closing deadline. Templates are starting points — everything is editable before you share the link.
What is the difference between Multiple Choice and Ranked Choice?
Multiple choice asks each person to pick one option. Ranked choice lets voters drag-rank all options in preference order — an instant runoff algorithm then finds the true consensus winner even when votes are split. Ranked choice is better for decisions where multiple options are popular and you need a clear winner, like feature prioritisation or topic selection.
Can I save my favourite templates?
Bookmark this page to keep the full library accessible. Each poll you create gets a unique shareable link you can bookmark. The Duplicate Poll feature (Pro plan) lets you copy any existing poll to reuse it with a fresh vote count.
