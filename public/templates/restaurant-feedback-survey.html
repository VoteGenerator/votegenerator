FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this restaurant feedback survey template free?
Yes. Free forever with no signup from you or your customers. Up to 100 responses free. Pro ($19/month) allows 10,000 responses — better for busy venues collecting feedback continuously.
What questions should a restaurant feedback survey include?
The most valuable restaurant feedback questions cover: overall experience, food quality, service, atmosphere, value for money, likelihood to recommend (NPS), return visit intent, and open improvement feedback. This template covers all eight dimensions.
How do I share this survey with customers?
Share via QR code on table cards, receipts, or menus. Add the link to your post-visit email. Include it in your Google or TripAdvisor listing. No login required from customers — they scan, click, and respond immediately in their browser.
Does this work for cafes, bars, and food trucks?
Yes. Works for any hospitality venue — restaurants, cafes, bars, pop-ups, food trucks, and hotel dining. All 8 questions are fully editable. Replace "restaurant" with "cafe" or "venue" before sharing. The feedback dimensions apply universally.
How do I use customer feedback to improve my restaurant?
Focus on the gap between your highest and lowest scores. If food quality scores 4.5 but service scores 3.2, service is your priority. Question 8 open text reveals the specific "one thing" customers wish you'd change. Aggregate 20+ responses before drawing conclusions.
Can I track satisfaction trends over time?
Free accounts see current results. Pro ($19/month) includes response timeline analytics — seeing whether scores improve after staffing changes, menu updates, or interior redesigns. The data that proves your improvements are working.
